Bio

About Bryan Persad

I have spent more than 17 years inside the trenches of email marketing and digital growth, working with e-commerce, subscription and education brands across multiple ESPs and stacks.

My work sits at the intersection of strategy and infrastructure: domain and sending setup, segmentation and lifecycle flows, analytics and attribution. I care about clean data, clear messaging and deliverability that holds up under real volume.

Today I help leadership and growth teams see the full picture. Not just opens and clicks, but how email, SMS and lifecycle programs tie into revenue, retention, LTV and long term brand equity.

Services

Email, lifecycle and deliverability work

I partner with brands as a hybrid strategist and operator, mapping lifecycle journeys, fixing infrastructure and building execution systems your team can actually run.

That usually means diagnosing what is really happening with your domain, audience and analytics, then designing a simple roadmap that respects constraints and still moves key metrics.

Common focus areas include:

  • Lifecycle architecture, welcome and post-purchase flows
  • Deliverability, domain and reputation repair
  • ESP migrations and multi-brand setups
  • GA4 and attribution frameworks for email and SMS
  • Testing roadmaps tied to revenue, LTV and retention
