Meet the Author

Hi, I’m Bryan Persad — an entrepreneur, strategist, and digital marketing veteran with 17+ years of hands-on experience in email marketing, SEO, SEM, and social media strategy. I’ve led retention and growth efforts for global SaaS companies, ecommerce powerhouses, and high-impact DTC brands, helping them reach their audience and scale sustainably.

Throughout my career, I’ve been passionate about more than just open rates and ROAS — I’ve been focused on clarity, intentionality, and building systems that actually work. Whether it's optimizing lifecycle marketing or launching performance-driven funnels, I bring both technical knowledge and creative perspective to the table.

That same mindset inspired me to become an entrepreneur. I founded Build Better Emails and Build Better Growth to help businesses and founders navigate their digital marketing with confidence, precision, and clarity.

In 2025, I authored the book Change Your Mind. Change the Game — a guide for creatives, leaders, and aspiring entrepreneurs who are ready to break from the old narratives and start changing the script they were handed.

You can connect with me on LinkedIn or explore more of what I do right here on this site.

The Book

"Change Your Mind, Change the Game by Bryan Persad is more than just a mindset book—it's a transformational guide for anyone ready to break free from autopilot and step into aligned, purposeful action. If you’ve ever felt stuck, burned out, or like you’re living a version of life that doesn’t reflect your true potential, this book is your roadmap to change.

Drawing from his own journey from burnout to breakthrough, Bryan empowers readers to rewrite the story society handed them and build a life on their own terms. Inside, you’ll find powerful mindset shifts, practical strategies, and real-world insights designed to help you lead, grow, and create with intention.

Whether you're an aspiring entrepreneur, creator, or professional seeking clarity and momentum, Change Your Mind, Change the Game offers a proven framework to unlock the life—and impact—you were meant for.
